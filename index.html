Privacy Policy – HydraTrack This Privacy Policy applies to the mobile application “HydraTrack” developed by Rania Amara. 1. Purpose of the Application HydraTrack is a wellness and health application designed to help users optimize and maintain healthy hydration habits through personalized intake targets, intake logging, historical data analysis, and automated smart reminders. 2. Information Usage & Core Features To provide a highly customized and reliable health-tracking experience, the app utilizes the following features: Authentication: To create, secure, and sync your personalized tracking profile. Hydration Algorithms: Personalized calculations utilizing biological inputs (weight, gender, physical activity level, and climate environment) to establish dynamic daily water intake targets. Progress Analytics: Logging capabilities to record your historical water consumption volume, daily completion percentages, goal streaks, and weekly tracking trends. Smart Reminders: System-level scheduled local notifications configured to prompt healthy hydration intervals throughout your active hours. 3. Data Collection & Third-Party Services HydraTrack uses Google Firebase and native mobile plugins to deliver a seamless cross-platform experience. Unlike local-only apps, core data is synchronized securely to the cloud to ensure consistency across your devices: Personal & Account Data: We collect your name and email address securely via Firebase Authentication to provision your account profile. Biological Parameters: Parameters such as your weight, gender, daily physical activity index, climate profile, and reminder schedules are processed to dynamically calibrate your daily target formula. Activity Logs: Historical water log entries (timestamped milliliter inputs and goal targets achieved) are stored reliably in Cloud Firestore. Security & Infrastructure: We do not have access to your personal passwords. All sensitive authentication traffic is isolated and managed securely by Google's dedicated infrastructure. 4. Data Sharing & Third Parties We maintain a strict stance on data privacy. We do not sell, trade, rent, or lease your personal data or health tracking metrics to advertisers or unauthorized third-party marketing companies. Your data is handled exclusively by: Google Firebase: For secure data cloud hosting, secure database state management, and real-time user authentication. Google Play Services / Apple App Store: For native application bundle distribution, update delivery, and baseline performance telemetry. 5. Health & Medical Disclaimer HydraTrack provides health utility tracking and hydration calculation metrics for informational and lifestyle optimization purposes only. The application: Does not provide professional medical advice, diagnoses, clinical evaluations, or healthcare treatments. Is not a medical device. Is not a substitute for professional healthcare consultations or medical guidance from a qualified doctor. Users with chronic health conditions (e.g., kidney disease, cardiovascular issues) requiring specific restricted fluid limitations must consult a physician rather than relying on automated app targets. 6. Children’s Privacy HydraTrack operates strictly as a wellness tracking utility and does not knowingly collect personal identifiable information from children under the age of 13. The application complies with all COPPA and GDPR-K standards regarding the protection of minors. 7. Data Deletion Rights Your data belongs entirely to you. You maintain the right to request the total permanent deletion of your profile account, account credentials, configurations, and all historical hydration intake data stored within our Firebase databases at any time. 8. Data Retention Policy We retain your personal data, biological parameters, and historical hydration logs only for as long as your account remains active and open. This data is necessary to maintain your personalized tracking profile and show you your historical trends. If you choose to request deletion via email, your personal information, authentication credentials, and tracking history will be permanently and securely deleted from our live Firebase databases within 30 days of the request. Backup copies of database files are automatically overwritten and destroyed within a standard retention cycle not exceeding 60 days. 9. Changes to This Policy This Privacy Policy may be updated occasionally to reflect changes in mobile software functionality, platform architecture updates, or evolving legal frameworks. 10.
